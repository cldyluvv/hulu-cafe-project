GET TO KNOW US

About Hulu Cafe

Hulu Cafe is a cozy modern café that brings together premium matcha drinks, freshly baked pastries, and comforting meals in a relaxing atmosphere. Every item on our menu is carefully prepared using high-quality ingredients to ensure the best taste in every bite and sip. Whether you are here to study, catch up with friends, or simply unwind, Hulu Cafe offers the perfect space for every moment.

Our Story

Hulu Cafe is a local Malaysian cafe that focuses on matcha, handcrafted beverages, pastries, pasta, western dishes and desserts. The cafe is known for its cozy atmosphere, aesthetic interior and work-friendly environment.

Hulu Cafe provides a comfortable space for customers to enjoy food, drinks, study sessions, casual meetups and relaxing cafe visits.

Experience

Hulu Cafe offers customers a comfortable dining experience with quality drinks, delicious meals and a calm environment. The cafe is suitable for students, young adults, families and customers who want to spend time working, studying or socialising.

Customers can enjoy premium matcha drinks, coffee, desserts and meals while experiencing a modern cafe setting.

Mission / Vision

Mission

To provide customers with quality food, premium matcha drinks, comfortable service and a welcoming cafe experience.

Vision

To become a well-known local cafe brand that brings people together through good food, drinks and meaningful cafe experiences.
